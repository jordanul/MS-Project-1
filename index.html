FAQs

How does it work?

We at Kae believe that when you buy anything, at any time, from anywhere - that offers a loyalty card, that loyalty should be rewarded.

So we've partnered with your favourite retailers, on and offline to give you just that.

All you have to do is sign up, add your card to your chosen service, and Bob just might be your Uncle (He prefers Robert).
You can even have a physical card if you'd like.

What is a Kae card?

Kae is a scheme that allows delivery service customers to collectively link their loyalty cards to their respective retailers.

Meaning you can shop freely from home or (almost) anywhere you can think of, with the knowledge your loyalty points will always be going exactly where they need to be:

Straight to you.
